LiverAId - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2, 2025
Information: This privacy policy explains how the LiverAId system collects, processes, and protects user data.

1. What Data We Collect

Data We Store:

  • User account information: Doctor name, surname, title, email address, specialty
  • Test parameters: Temporarily during analysis only (deleted after analysis)

Data We Don't Store:

  • Patient test results or analysis results
  • Patient identity information
  • Uploaded documents (sent directly to Google's Gemini)

2. Data Usage Purposes

  • Liver disease risk assessment
  • AI-assisted medical analysis
  • System security and performance
  • User support

3. Third-Party Sharing

Important: Only anonymous medical parameters are shared with the following third-party services (provided documents are stripped of identifying information):
  • OpenAI/OpenRouter API: AI chatbot and result evaluations
  • Google Gemini API: Document OCR processing

These services have their own privacy policies and are outside our control.

4. Document Upload and Data Processing

Important Information: About the document upload feature:

When uploading documents, you must remove personal patient information (names, ID numbers, patient numbers, etc.) beforehand. Uploaded documents are sent directly to Google's Gemini service and are not stored in our system. We do not assume any legal responsibility for the storage and processing of uploaded documents.

5. Data Security

  • Secure communication via HTTPS
  • Regular security updates
  • Access control and authorization

6. Data Retention Period

  • User account information: Until account deletion
  • Uploaded documents: Sent to third-party services (Google's Gemini) and subject to their retention policies

7. User Rights

Under data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

  • To know whether your data is being processed
  • To request correction of your data
  • To request deletion of your data
  • To know third parties to whom your data is transferred

8. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be changed without prior notice. Users are advised to check the current policy regularly.

Consent: By using the system, you declare that you have read, understood, and accepted the above privacy policy.
